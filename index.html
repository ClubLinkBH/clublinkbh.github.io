About ClubLink

ClubLink is an innovative organization dedicated to enriching student culture within the Bloomfield Hills School District. Our mission is to empower student clubs by providing them with the resources, support, and connectivity they need to thrive.

Ultimately, we're committed to building a vibrant, connected community where every club and student can reach their full potential. By fostering collaboration and introducing several new tools, ClubLink aims to revolutionize the club experience.
